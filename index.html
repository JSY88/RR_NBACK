🧠 Relational Integration Training

Challenge your brain with this engaging number sequence game!

🎯 The Challenge

Watch a sequence of numbers appear one at a time. Your task is to compare the differences between numbers!

🎮 How to Play

1-Back Level:

Compare: (current - previous) vs (previous - two before)

Equal Example: [5, 3, 1] → (1-3) vs (3-5) → Both = 2 ✓

Different Example: [5, 3, 2] → (2-3) vs (3-5) → 1 vs 2 ✗

2-Back Level:

Compare: (current - two before) vs (previous - three before)

Equal Example: [2, 5, 8, 11] → (11-5) vs (8-2) → Both = 6 ✓

Different Example: [2, 5, 8, 10] → (10-5) vs (8-2) → 5 vs 6 ✗

How to Respond:

✓ Press "Equal" if the differences match

✓ Press "Different" if the differences don't match

✓ Numbers appear one at a time - remember them!

⌨️ Controls

Default: Space: Start | Y: Equal | N: Different | Escape: Close settings | Ctrl+S: Open settings

Use the ⚙️ button to customize hotkeys and training settings!
